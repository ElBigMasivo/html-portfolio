The best movies according to Manuel

My top 3 movies of all time.

BloodSport

I love this movie because of the way it demonstrates how passion
and dedication can fuel people to overcome the most challenging
obstacles placed before them.


The Devil's Advocate

This a great example of how people tend to get lost due to vanity
and the lust for money and power.


Saw

Watch as a maniacal villain set's people in traps as a way to hold
them accountable for their wrongdoings and allow them
freedom but only at the cost of.....a piece of the puzzle.
